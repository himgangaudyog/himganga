Advantage of uPVC

  • Weatherproof: uPVC is not easily affected by climatic changes unlike other substances like wood.
  • Limited Maintenance: Since the material is so durable, once in place, uPVC widnows and doors would need very little maintenance. In fact uPVC window frames do not need painting or sealing and are easily cleaned with water and detergent, significantly reducing the maintenance required over their lifetime. The material does not rot and it is also resistant to corrosion and salt erosion.
  • Noise insulation – Double glazed uPVC windows and doors are able to cut down noise by as much as 70%.
  • Environmentally friendly – uPVC can be recycled as often as 10 times. In fact in Europe, uPVC windows and doors can be, and are recycled at the end of life.
